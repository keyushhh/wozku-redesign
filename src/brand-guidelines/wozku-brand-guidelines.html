Typography

Our typography is modern, readable and built for digital experiences. Each typeface has a specific role in creating clarity and hierarchy. We use a combination of three families with carefully selected fallbacks for every platform.

Primary — Space GroteskUsed for headlines and key messaging.
Secondary — SatoshiUsed for body copy and interface text.
Tertiary — JetBrains MonoUsed for technical content, labels and data.

Visual Language

These key elements shape how we express the Wozku brand across all touchpoints. Use them consistently to create a clear, professional and unified brand presence.

Grid & Spacing

8pt spacing system for layouts and consistent rhythm.

Corner Radius

16px is our default radius for cards and UI surfaces.

Iconography

Rounded, outline icons with 1.5–2px stroke weight.

Illustration Style

Clean, minimal illustrations using geometric shapes and brand colours.

Gradients & Effects

Soft gradients and shadows used sparingly to create depth and emphasis.

Photography Style

Authentic, natural imagery focused on people, collaboration and infrastructure.

Logo Clear Space

To ensure visibility and impact, always keep clear space around the logo. The clear space is defined by the height of the logo mark (X). No text, graphics, or other elements should enter this zone.
